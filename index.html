About

Hello, my name is Andrius Alimas, I am a Full Stack Web Developer. I created this responsive web site, because my course challenge - ask me to create Responsive Club Web Site. I am more happy to create and learn a long. When I have free time I like play strategy games, and one of favorite game is AOW – Age Of Wonders.

This was one of childhood game a played a lot. It’s like Civilization combined with Heroes might and magic one of the best games come to one. Of course this game updated a lot and was created lots of mods which improve game a lot. I chose this mods combined:

1. AOW 1.4
2. 3-in-1 Pack
3. Patch MP Evolution 1.5
4. AOW 1.5
5. Custom Resources set Dwiggs 1.4_Patch

If you want to join this club you have to be very good on strategy, because here is the best players gather. As well you have to follow the rules which as well balance the game and more challenging, every single game will be different and you will enter to fantasy world where you have to made best decision to your race.

Read Rules, because its mandatory to follow. All history battle will be recorded in nice table, and some of screenshot will be taken or even videos. All race will be randomly selected and email to you before beginning ranking game. I hope you will enjoy club and have fun, please share this website, more players better. Our mission to create ranking and to see best strategy game play all around the world and compete and be awarded with a prize – this is our goal!
